## 💼 Featured Projects
### 🏦 Banking Analytics Fraud Detection

* Performed EDA on **20K+ banking transaction records**, uncovering customer behavioral segments and key drivers of financial product adoption.
* Applied **Cohort Analysis** and **RFM modeling** to identify key retention patterns, revealing cyclical reactivation peaks at the 1-month and 6-month marks.
* Segmented customers into distinct profiles via **Customer Lifetime Value (CLV)** evaluation, identifying high-potential VIP groups that drive the majority of revenue.
* Engineered transactional features and applied PCA (Principal Component Analysis) to reduce dimensionality, surfacing key variance factors to support strategic recommendations.
* Built an interactive Power BI dashboard translating segment-level financial insights into business-ready visuals accessible to non-technical stakeholders.
* **Tech Stack:** `Python`, `Power BI`, `Scikit-learn`, `Pandas`, `Machine Learning`.
* [View Repository](https://github.com/KhoiDang-KHDL/banking-analytics-fraud-detection.git))


### 📈 E-Commerce Funnel & Marketing Performance Analysis (TheLook)

Conducted end-to-end business performance analysis on a large-scale e-commerce dataset (100K+ users) to identify high-value channels and conversion bottlenecks.

### 🚅 UK Train Operations Analysis – Reducing Delays & Optimizing Refund Costs

Conducted end-to-end analysis on 31.6K+ UK train transactions to diagnose operational bottlenecks, optimize refund costs, and improve customer experience using data-driven insights.

* Analyzed 31.6K+ train transaction records to identify root causes of delays, cancellations, and refund costs.
* Performed data cleaning, feature engineering, and EDA using Python (Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib, Seaborn) and SQL.
* Discovered that >50% of delays were driven by controllable operational issues (signal, technical, staffing), heavily concentrated during peak hours.
* Proposed data-driven strategies that could reduce delay rate (15% → 5–10%) and refund cost (~26% → 15%).
* **Tech Stack:** `Python (Pandas, NumPy)`, `Data Visualization (PowerBI)`, `Exploratory Data Analysis`, `Statistical Analysis`.
* [View Repository](https://github.com/KhoiDang-KHDL/The-UK-Train-Analystics.git)

### 🩺 SAM2-DEB-UNet: Polyp Segmentation with SAM2 and Boundary-Guided Refinement

* Developed a boundary-aware dual-encoder framework based on SAM2 for robust segmentation of colorectal polyps and marine animals.
* Built a dual-encoder architecture using PyTorch, pairing SAM2 Hiera-L with a ConvNeXt-V2-Tiny auxiliary encoder via a Dense Glue fusion module to align multi-scale features.
* Incorporated a Boundary-Guided Head Refinement (BGHR) module to perform explicit edge prediction and edge-attentive refinement for improved boundary delineation.
* Evaluated the model across five polyp segmentation benchmarks and the RMAS marine animal dataset, utilizing a multi-component loss that combines structure, deep supervision, edge, and boundary losses.
* Achieved state-of-the-art results, surpassing the SAM2-UNet baseline on four out of five polyp datasets with an mDice of 0.935 on Kvasir and reaching 0.774 mIoU on the RMAS dataset.
* **Tech Stack:** `Python`, `PyTorch`, `SAM2`, `ConvNeXt-V2`, `Computer Vision`, `Medical Image Segmentation`.
* [View Repository](https://github.com/dryi37/Project_DS200.git)
